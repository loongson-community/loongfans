The Loongson 2K3000 AI Evaluation Board is a development board from Loongson Technology based on the 2K3000-i platform.

## Key Specifications

| Category | Specification |
|------|------|
| Manufacturer | Loongson Technology |
| Form Factor | 168×126mm |
| Processor | 2K3000 @ 2.2GHz |
| Memory | DDR4 @ 3200MT/s (1 × SODIMM) |
| Interfaces (USB)  | USB 3.0 (2 × onboard, 1 × pin header), USB 2.0 (3 × pin headers) |
| Interfaces (Storage) | 1 × NVMe (PCIe 3.0 x4), 1 × microSD card slot, 1 × SATA 3.0 |
| Interfaces (Network) | 2 × GbE |
| Interfaces (Video) | 1 × HDMI, 1 × eDP |
| Interfaces (Other) | 1 × M.2 Key E slot (PCIe + USB), 1 × 3.5mm headphone/microphone jack, 1 × SIM card slot (available for 4G LTE), 2 × CAN-FD, 2 × serial port (RS-232), 2 × RS-485, 1 × UART TTL |

†: One of the USB-C ports is power-only and does not support data transfer.
