The LoongArch biweekly is a community meeting organized by Loongson hobbyists. The meeting agenda includes development progress reports on upstream, various Linux distributions, and other system projects, community affairs reports, as well as contributor discussions and Q&A sessions.

The biweekly is held every other Sunday at 2:00 PM (UTC+8) on Tencent Meeting, and is simultaneously livestreamed on Bilibili [@龙芯爱好者社区](https://space.bilibili.com/70360929). Offline activities are also arranged from time to time.
